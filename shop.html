Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our watches

What makes SUED watches unique?

Every SUED watch is handcrafted in Nairobi using premium materials and traditional techniques. We blend Kenyan heritage with contemporary design.

How do I choose the right size?

Most watches have adjustable straps (18-22cm). For precise sizing, use our printable size guide or contact our stylists for assistance.

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns for unworn watches in original packaging. Exchanges are free within Kenya.

How long does shipping take?

Nairobi: 1-2 business days. Other Kenya locations: 3-5 days. International: 7-14 days depending on location.

Are the watches water resistant?

Most leather watches are splash resistant. Our Safari Explorer collection is water resistant to 50 meters (5ATM).

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! All orders come with free luxury gift packaging. You can also add a personalized message.
